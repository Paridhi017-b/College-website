Sankalp:A Rotaract Club is a group of dedicated members whose stated purpose is to provide humanitarian service and to advance goodwill and peace around us. Our main goal is to serve the community and make a positive difference in the lives of others. It's all about creating a better world through acts of kindness and service.
As part of the global effort, our Rotaract club is actively contributing to peace and international understanding. It's great to be engaged in various activities like social, professional, and community events. By organizing events and donating necessary items, we are making a positive impact and taking care of the needs of the society

HOSTEL FACILITIES

In order to support out-station students, Shri Vaishnav Shekshanik and Parmarthik Trust has built separate hostels for boys and girls in the central part of Indore. Admissions in these hostels are on "first come first serve basis". In addition to the above, there is a private boys' hostel located opposite to the institute.

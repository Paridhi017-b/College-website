Our Vision is to create an educational environment that engages deep intellectual, moral and spiritual stimulation, thereby nurturing leadership.


Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya [SVVV], Indore Madhya Pradesh was established in the year 2007 as Private University Educational Institute to provide higher education to build a career in the field of Engineering, Management and Humanities. SVVV is approved by the reputed UGC. Management of SVVV Indore is working under Purushottamdas Pasari (Chancellor).

From the Desk of the CHANCELLOR

Far from the hustle and bustle of city life, in the midst of greenery we work hard at SVVV to see to it that our students receive a high quality education with all the finest possible supervision, guidance, and support of our competent faculty. We make sure that students acquire not only subject knowledge but also develop a comprehensive set of skills to prepare them to face the real world and make them industry ready.
Keeping in mind the importance of research in today's world, apart from the curricular and extracurricular activities, the university also organizes a large member of national and international conferences, seminars, and workshops. Such activities expose students to a wide range of new knowledge and experiences and instill in them the qualities of leadership and teamwork, which are beneficial for their future endeavors. I am pleased to welcome the new batch of students and wish them all a fulfilling and empowering journey towards excellence at SVVV.
